NUNA - A Case Study

NUNA started as an NFT project, but has quickly become a one-stop-shop for empowering artists and providing resources and services for NFT investors. As a top 20 project on Stellar (by TVL) NUNA has quickly become one of the leading projects due to it's focus on community management and ecosystem rewards.

NUNA issues rewards to the following liquidity pools:

  • NUNA/XLM ($48,000 TVL)
  • NUNA/AQUA ($2,600 TVL)

NUNA also offers Staking services, where users can earn hourly rewards when they lock up tokens for a month or longer. The Staking app can be found at this link. It currently secures over $38,000 in locked funds.

Services from Cordillera allow the project team at NUNA to focus more on the design and scope of the products and services they want to offer their users, while Cordillera handles the technical implementation and automation processes. Even in the bear market, NUNA sits at over $85,000 in TVL between their AMMs and Staking program.
