Astro Shiba - A Case Study

Astro Shiba is a new memecoin project on Stellar. However, that doesn't mean it's not useful. The team at Astro Shiba is attempting to create a token that is able to compete with larger Liquidity Reward applications like AQUA.

Astro Shiba issues rewards to the following liquidity pools:

  • XSHIB/XLM ($1,300 TVL)
  • XSHIB/NUNA ($6,100 TVL)
  • XSHIB/ASHIB ($5,800 TVL)
  • XSHIB/USDC ($170 TVL)
  • XSHIB/LSP ($250 TVL)
  • XSHIB/AQUA ($300 TVL)
  • ASHIB/NUNA ($3,250 TVL)
  • ASHIB/XLM ($770 TVL)

ASHIB is still young, and is still in the process of getting it's name out there. But because of it's partnerships with important projects, and quick deployment of liquidity rewards, Astro Shiba is shaping up to become a solid project.

Astro Shiba currently operates 8 pools on Cordillera's Liquidity Reward System, with plans to add 3 more by next month. This has allowed Astro Shiba to raise powerful liquidity it can use to build it's community and partnerships.
